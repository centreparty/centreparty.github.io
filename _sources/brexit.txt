Life After Brexit
=================

.. image:: _static/childrendancing.jpg

The British people voted to leave the European Union on the 23
June 2016. 

The UK must leave before the European Parliament election of
June 2019. The political establishment in the UK and in the EU is
likely to keep arguing about how to leave until that final deadline.

Let's just fast forward through that and think about life after
Brexit. What kind of independent country do we want to be?
